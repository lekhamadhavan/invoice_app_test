Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

This application connects to QuickBooks Online via n8n to facilitate internal event production workflows. We only access Invoice and Customer data required for show scheduling and inventory tracking.

2. Data Usage

Data is used exclusively for internal business automation between QuickBooks, n8n, and Airtable.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share any QuickBooks data with third parties. Data is stored securely within our private Airtable and n8n environment.

4. Security

Access tokens are encrypted and stored in a private n8n instance.
